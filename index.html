Privacy Policy - Classroom Buddy

Privacy Policy

Classroom Buddy

Overview

Classroom Buddy is a local-first classroom management app designed for teachers. We respect your privacy and do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any external servers.

Data Storage

All data entered into Classroom Buddy is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Student names
  • Attendance records
  • Performance feedback
  • Group assignments
  • Photos or avatars (if used)

No Accounts or Cloud Sync

Classroom Buddy does not require user accounts and does not sync data to any cloud service. All information remains on the device.

Camera & Photos

If you choose to use the camera or photo library, this is only for selecting student profile images. Images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded.

Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or transmit any user data to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Classroom Buddy is designed for educational environments and may be used in classrooms with minors. No personal data is collected or transmitted externally.
