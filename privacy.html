Privacy Policy

Classroom Buddy

Overview

Classroom Buddy is a local-first classroom management app for teachers. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All data remains on your device only:

  • Student names
  • Attendance records
  • Performance feedback
  • Groups
  • Photos or avatars

No Accounts

Classroom Buddy does not require accounts or online sign-in.

Camera & Photos

Camera and photo library access is only used for selecting student profile images. Images remain stored locally on your device.

Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Designed for educational use. No external data collection occurs.
